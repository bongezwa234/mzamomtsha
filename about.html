BIOGRAPHY OF THE SCHOOL


Welcome to Mzamomtsha Primary Intermediate School information page. Mzamomtsha Primary School is an Intermediate School located in City Of Cape Town – Western Cape. Mzamomtsha Primary School is a valid, registered and prosperous school under the leadership of the Principal S.G Kuze, 9 teachers and 530 learners. Mzamomtsha Primary School is an Urban Public Intermediate School located in Driftsands City Of Cape Town.



Principal Mr S.G Khuze

Headmaster


The principal of Mzamomtsha Primary School, Mr Simphiwe G Khuze studied at the University of the Western Cape (UWC),from 1990-1992 doing Bachelor of Art(B.A), History and African Languages. He furthered his studies by doing Higher Diploma in Education in 1993. Not giving up, he continued with Bachelor of Education (BEd) in 1994 Educational Management.
He taught at Mfuleni Combined School(1994-1998) and Manzomthombo S.S.S(1998-2000). He was appointed as the Head of Department as well the Deputy Principal. He was a History NSC Marker, Chief Marker and an Internal Moderator. He became a principal of Mzamomtsha Primary School earlier in January 2018 till present.
Till death do Simphiwe and Education apart!

Learners

Everyone faces challenges in life, but the capacity to overcome hardships, learn from mistakes, and forge ahead in the face of immense adversity is a quality inherent in most successful individuals. The obstacles facing Mzamomtsha students are daunting daily exposure to drugs, alcoholism, hunger, abuse, negative role models, violence and suffering but every day our students come to school, learn, and set their sights on a better tomorrow. They have dreams, and while at Mzamomtsha their aspiration is to make those dreams become reality. We celebrate their determination and courage. They are breaking through the barriers of poverty to build a new and better life for themselves and a future for their families. They are our heroes, and role models for others to follow.
